Gamma distribution with shape parameter $\alpha >0$ and rate parameter $\lambda>0$ with density:
$$f_X(x)=\frac{\lambda^\alpha x^{\alpha-1}e^{-\lambda x}}{\Gamma(\alpha)}\qquad (x>0)$$
If $\alpha=1$, then the gamma distribution reduces to the exponential distribution. 
Properties of $\Gamma$:
- Recurrence
$$\Gamma(\alpha+1)=\alpha\Gamma(\alpha)$$
- Integral Values
$$\Gamma(n+1)=n!$$
$$E[X]=\frac{\alpha}{\lambda},\qquad Var(X)=\frac{\alpha}{\lambda^2}$$
